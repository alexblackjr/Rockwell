Songwriter/Producer of the future

Ron Rockwell sits comfortably on the notion that he is unable to box himself into one style. Producing everything from Hip Hop to Rock, R&B to Dancehall, Soca to Kompa, and Pop to House, he represent the culmination of the urban experience. Absorbing the streets, he bends the said genres to fit his purpose. Looking to give artists a fresh sound, he creates with an emphasis on a strong publishing catalog. These features establish Ron Rockwell as a production powerhouse that labels can rely on for both quality and an understanding of the market. He brings a unique musicianship that accumulates to this complete circle. The bottom line: Hot beats. You name it, he does it (write, produce)...... and well. Hood felt grit, edgy, bounce-heavy syncopation, to lush, layered and well arranged songs are what you can expect from Ron Rockwell.
